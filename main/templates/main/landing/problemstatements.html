GPS enables Destination tracker System

Code : XWH_P01

A Real-time destination information solution to plan and visit, on the go.

The proposed solution will be a mobile application that helps the tourists to access the live information of a destination to be planned/visited by the tourists. The solution should help the tourists to track the live status of a destination point and plan their visit and also the departments in providing updated information to intended tourists. The system should automatically update the timings and current status of the destination (unless otherwise changed by the Destination Manager of the concerned department). The system should also collect data for the department to analyze the number of tourists visited and its trends.

Unified Ticket Management System

Code : XWH_P02

A web and mobile app-based ticket management system of the department of tourism and DTPC.

The proposed Unified Ticket Management System, is a centralized web and mobile app system that allows the department and its internal stakeholders to generate, manage and track the tickets for various events, activities, and attractions. This includes tasks such as issuing tickets to customers (for multiple activities), tracking tickets and their sales, and managing their availability. The system will also support a parking assistance system in selected destinations as well. The solution can help in improving the efficiency and effectiveness of the department and ensure that tourists (domestic/international) have a positive experience at the destination.

GIS-enabled Facility Management Solution

Code : XWH_P03

GIS-enabled system to manage and rate the various facilities/amenities in a tourist destination.

Tourism department provides various facilities to tourists visiting various destinations. The proposed solution will be a GIS-enabled destination management system, which helps the tourists to identify a specific destination and the facilities made available. It also provides the users to rate the facilities, based on their experience and also in providing their feedback (as text and image uploads). The system should also automatically analyze the maximum usage of a specific facility/destination spot for suggestions. The system should automatically / manually create a digital map of the area that includes information about the location of various attractions and amenities, as well as the surrounding area. This can be useful for tourists as they plan their visit, as it allows them to see the layout of the destination and locate nearby points of interest.

The system should also help the department to analyze the facilities provide, for quality assurance and the satisfaction level of the users. Process monitoring, such as tracking the maintenance and cleanliness of washrooms, can also be an important aspect of destination rating, as clean and well-maintained facilities can contribute to a positive experience for tourists. By tracking and monitoring these factors, the destination can identify areas for improvement and take steps to enhance the visitor experience.
