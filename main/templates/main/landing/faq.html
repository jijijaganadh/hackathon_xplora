How do I Register for Xplora-WINHACK?
Xplora is a live 30 Hrs long coding challenge specifically for women, in the field of Tourism.For registering in the event, you can visit Xplora WINHACK Registration , provide your details (and of your team) and also provide the details of the solution you have envisaged.

Registration Process:

  1. Create an account
  2. Submit Team information (of Team lead and supporting member)
  3. Complete your profile including updating the details of the mentor, if any
  4. Select a problem statement
  5. Submit your solution
  6. Complete the registration process.
Xplora WIN HACK is only open to teams? Can an individual participate?
You can participate in this event as a team or as an individual contributor, providing your solutions to the problem statements, problem statements. For those participating as a team, the team lead needs to do the registration process. Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
Is it possible to change the team members after registration?
Members of the team can be changed up until the initial evaluation process. Once the initial evaluation is complete, the registered team will be treated as final. However, there is no provision to change the team lead.
What are the selection criteria for the final hackathon?
Evaluation criteria will include the novelty of the idea, simplicity, clarity, cost effectiveness, social relevance, and viability of the project.
What is the last date of submission of solution and Grand Finale ?
Last date for submission is 17th February 2023  10th March 2023 and Grand Finale conducted on 18th, 19th March 2023.
What are the Prize for Winners?
The top teams from each problem statement will be rewarded with first & second cash prizes worth Rs. 20,000/- & Rs.10,000/- respectively.
