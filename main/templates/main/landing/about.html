Xplora-WinHack

18th, 19th March 2023

Xplora Win Hack is a two-day program run by ICFOSS (International Centre For Free and Open-Source Software) in association with the Tourism Department of Kerala that brings together women programmers, designers, and entrepreneurs to collaborate on building innovative technology solutions. The event will be a continuous coding challenge lasting for 30 hours on the 18th & 19th of March 2023 at ICFOSS where participants work in teams to create and present their projects. The goal of a women's hackathon is to empower and support women in the tech industry and promote diversity and inclusion in the field.

ICFOSS is the outcome of a decade of work by Free Software enthusiasts, advocates, developers, and supporters in the state of Kerala and outside. Recognizing the strides made by the FOSS community in Kerala, the Government of Kerala implemented the International Centre for Free and Open Source Software (ICFOSS) in 2009. ICFOSS today carries out a number of FOSS-related activities in a model that brings together academia, industry, government, and the FOSS Community.
Want to know more about us? Visit us at https://icfoss.in.
