Things to remember before registration

Xplora-WIN HACK (Women in Hackathon) is a programme run by ICFOSS that encourages young women to develop confidence and a sense of independence. The event will be a continuous coding challenge lasting for 30 hours on the 25th & 26th of February 2023 at ICFOSS.
Guidelines for the hackathon are listed below:

Registration

The students can register as a team or can participate as individuals (without a team).
The registration of teams must be done on (website) by the Team Leader only.
The registration process will begin on January 15th, 2023.
Inter-college teams are not allowed.

Team Formation

A team should consist of at most three members.
Each team should nominate one member as the Team Lead. The selected Team Lead may not change his position during the hackathon period.
The Team Lead will be the primary point of contact for the team during the hackathon.
each team will have the option of selecting one mentor from the industry or academia.
A mentor is expected to have relevant experience of Industry/ Academic experience to qualify.

Problem Statement

Select the most suitable problem you think you can effectively solve using the available resources.
A team can select only one problem statement and will not be allowed to change once chosen.
The teams have to propose a business model for the selected problem statement, and the solution has to be uploaded to the website (presentation along with proper documentation) in PDF format only.
Teams are free to use any open-source libraries and frameworks of their choice.

Idea Submission

The ideas can be submitted only by the team leader by logging to the online portal. Submission dates should be strictly followed.
The last date for submitting the ideas is 31st January 2023. No exceptions will be made on the submission date.
Project design, preprocessing tasks, and data collection (if required), can be done prior to the start date of the hackathon.

Shortlisted Ideas Announcement

Notification about selected teams will be put up on our portal and will also be sent to their respective email Ids.
Evaluation factors will include the novelty of the idea, knowledge of the subject matter, derived solution/model proposed, data aspects, and social relevance of the solution.

Grand Finale

The Final Hackathon (Xplora-WIN HACK) will be a continuous 30-hour Coding challenge taking place offline on 25th, 26th February 2023.
The best three teams will be selected from among the participants through evaluation.
All team members/individuals must be present at the venue for the entire duration of the event.
To ensure a level playing field for all contestants, all coding activities must be conducted at the venue only.
